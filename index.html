Welcome to Taniti Island

See the beauty of Taniti island. Taniti has sandy beaches and beautiful rainforests for all to explore.

About Taniti Island

Tanitis is a small island with a diverse landscape. There are beaches with a small harbor and rainforests. The island does have an active volcano. Taniti is home to 20000 indigenous people. Tourism is a big part of Tanitis economy bringing in people from all around the world.

What to Do on Taniti Island

Explore the Beaches

Relax on the beach and enjoy the sun around yellow leaf bay.

Adventure Awaits

Hike through the rainforest or explore the summit of mount Taniti volcano.

Cultural Experiences

Come see the Taniti museum. Explore the local cuisine at the resturants.

Water Activities

Snorkel in the blue lagoon. Take a boat trip around the island or go on a fishing tour.

Plan Your Visit

Getting to Taniti is easy, wanting to leave is the hard part! Get a start on planning your trip to Taniti. We would love to be the place you take your next vacation!
